## 组合示例

### 雨夜霓虹

```
Heavy rain pouring down on neon-lit city street at night.
Colorful neon signs reflecting on wet asphalt, creating pools
of pink, blue, and purple light. Raindrops visible in the glow
of streetlights. Steam rising from manholes. Low-key lighting
with high contrast between neon highlights and deep shadows.
```

### 黄金时刻森林

```
Dense forest bathed in golden hour sunlight. Warm amber rays
filtering through leaves, creating dappled light patterns on
forest floor. Volumetric light beams visible through morning mist.
Soft shadows, low contrast, dreamy ethereal atmosphere.
```

### 废弃工厂月光

```
Abandoned warehouse interior lit only by cold blue moonlight
streaming through broken skylights. Single flickering fluorescent
tube casting harsh light in corner. Dust particles floating in
moonbeams. Deep shadows, high contrast, eerie unsettling mood.
```
