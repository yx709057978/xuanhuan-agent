# 镜头类型速查表

> 用于分镜脚本的镜头类型参考

---

## 按距离分类

### 远景 (Extreme Wide Shot / EWS)
- **用途**: 建立场景、展示环境规模
- **适用**: 开场、场景转换、史诗感
- **Sora 描述词**: `extreme wide shot`, `establishing shot`, `aerial view`

### 全景 (Wide Shot / WS)
- **用途**: 展示角色与环境关系
- **适用**: 动作场景、群戏、追逐
- **Sora 描述词**: `wide shot`, `full shot`, `long shot`

### 中景 (Medium Shot / MS)
- **用途**: 日常对话、角色互动
- **适用**: 对话、日常场景
- **Sora 描述词**: `medium shot`, `waist shot`, `mid shot`

### 中近景 (Medium Close-up / MCU)
- **用途**: 强调表情同时保留手势
- **适用**: 重要对话、情绪表达
- **Sora 描述词**: `medium close-up`, `chest shot`

### 特写 (Close-up / CU)
- **用途**: 强调表情、情绪、细节
- **适用**: 情绪高潮、重要反应
- **Sora 描述词**: `close-up`, `face shot`, `tight shot`

### 大特写 (Extreme Close-up / ECU)
- **用途**: 极度强调细节
- **适用**: 眼神、物品、关键道具
- **Sora 描述词**: `extreme close-up`, `detail shot`, `insert shot`

## 按角度分类

### 平视 (Eye Level)
- **用途**: 中性、客观视角
- **适用**: 日常场景、对话
- **Sora 描述词**: `eye level`, `neutral angle`

### 俯视 (High Angle)
- **用途**: 角色显得弱小、被压迫
- **适用**: 表现弱势、绝望
- **Sora 描述词**: `high angle`, `looking down`, `bird's eye view`

### 仰视 (Low Angle)
- **用途**: 角色显得强大、威严
- **适用**: 表现权威、威胁
- **Sora 描述词**: `low angle`, `looking up`, `hero shot`

### 荷兰角 (Dutch Angle)
- **用途**: 不安、混乱、紧张
- **适用**: 心理扭曲、危险场景
- **Sora 描述词**: `dutch angle`, `tilted frame`, `canted angle`

### 过肩镜头 (Over-the-Shoulder / OTS)
- **用途**: 对话场景、建立空间关系
- **适用**: 两人对话
- **Sora 描述词**: `over-the-shoulder shot`, `OTS`

### POV 主观镜头 (Point of View)
- **用途**: 代入角色视角
- **适用**: 紧张、恐怖、发现
- **Sora 描述词**: `POV shot`, `first-person view`, `subjective shot`

## 按运动分类

### 静止 (Static)
- **用途**: 稳定、庄重
- **适用**: 对话、沉思
- **Sora 描述词**: `static shot`, `locked off`, `fixed camera`

### 推进 (Push In / Dolly In)
- **用途**: 强调、紧张感增加
- **适用**: 重要时刻、情绪升级
- **Sora 描述词**: `push in`, `dolly in`, `slow zoom in`

### 拉远 (Pull Out / Dolly Out)
- **用途**: 揭示环境、孤独感
- **适用**: 结尾、揭示
- **Sora 描述词**: `pull out`, `dolly out`, `zoom out`

### 横移 (Tracking / Dolly)
- **用途**: 跟随角色移动
- **适用**: 行走、追逐
- **Sora 描述词**: `tracking shot`, `dolly shot`, `following shot`

### 摇镜 (Pan)
- **用途**: 展示环境、跟随动作
- **适用**: 环境介绍、视线跟随
- **Sora 描述词**: `pan left/right`, `horizontal pan`

### 升降 (Crane / Jib)
- **用途**: 史诗感、揭示
- **适用**: 开场、高潮
- **Sora 描述词**: `crane shot`, `rising shot`, `descending shot`

### 手持 (Handheld)
- **用途**: 紧张、真实感、混乱
- **适用**: 动作、追逐、恐怖
- **Sora 描述词**: `handheld`, `shaky cam`, `documentary style`

## 玄幻/动作场景常用组合

### 打斗场景
```
1. Wide establishing (全景建立双方位置)
2. Medium two-shot (中景对峙)
3. Close-up reaction (特写反应)
4. Wide action (全景动作)
5. Close-up impact (特写碰撞)
6. Low angle hero shot (仰视英雄镜头)
```

### 对话场景
```
1. Wide establishing (全景建立环境)
2. Medium two-shot (中景双人)
3. OTS on A (A的过肩镜头)
4. Close-up B reaction (B的特写反应)
5. OTS on B (B的过肩镜头)
6. Close-up A reaction (A的特写反应)
```

### 氛围/情绪场景
```
1. Extreme wide establishing (远景建立)
2. Slow push in to medium (缓慢推进到中景)
3. Close-up on detail (细节特写)
4. Close-up on face (面部特写)
```

### 揭示/震撼场景
```
1. POV approaching (主观接近)
2. Close-up reaction (特写反应)
3. Wide reveal (全景揭示)
4. Low angle dramatic (仰视戏剧性)
```

## Sora 2 镜头描述示例

### 远景示例
```
Extreme wide shot of a ruined mountain temple at dusk.
Mist rolls through the valleys below. A lone figure stands
at the edge of a cliff, silhouetted against the orange sky.
```

### 特写示例
```
Close-up on a young man's face, dirt and blood on his cheek.
His eyes narrow with determination. Firelight flickers
across his features, casting dancing shadows.
```

### 动态镜头示例
```
Tracking shot following a warrior running through a bamboo forest.
Camera moves alongside him at eye level. Bamboo stalks blur past.
Sunlight filters through the canopy in golden streaks.
```
