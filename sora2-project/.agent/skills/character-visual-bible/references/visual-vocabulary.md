## 使用技巧

### 组合描述公式

```
[体型] + [年龄] + [性别] + [面部特征] + [发型发色] + [服装] + [配饰] + [状态]
```

### 示例组合

**现代都市女性**:
```
Slim young woman in her mid-twenties, oval face with high cheekbones, 
shoulder-length wavy chestnut brown hair, wearing a fitted black blazer 
over a white silk blouse, dark skinny jeans, silver hoop earrings, 
confident upright posture.
```

**奇幻战士**:
```
Tall muscular man in his thirties, square jaw with a slash scar across 
left cheek, short cropped black hair, wearing worn leather armor over 
chainmail, heavy sword strapped to back, weathered battle-hardened expression.
```

**赛博朋克角色**:
```
Lean androgynous figure, sharp angular features, half-shaved head with 
neon blue hair on one side, cybernetic eye implant glowing red, 
wearing a sleek black bodysuit with holographic circuit patterns, 
multiple ear piercings, detached cool expression.
```
